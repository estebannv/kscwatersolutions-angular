About Us

Mision

Offer comprehensive solutions to different needs related to the use and exploitation of surface and underground water in Costa Rica, either with the proper management of existing water sources or in the assessment and construction of new sources. We work diligently to guide our clients to make the right decisions in relation to water, which contribute to the functionality and design of their projects, without major complications.

Vision

Consolidate ourselves as a leading company in services for the use of water and its sources, both in the management of water as a fragile resource, legally protected, and in its correct management in the operational phase of use. Our commitment is to be recognized for professionalism, for the quality of our services and for our contribution to sustainable development.

Business Values

  • Closeness
  • Constancy
  • Social and environmental responsibility
  • Professionalism
  • Ethics
